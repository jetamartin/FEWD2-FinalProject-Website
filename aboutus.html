About Us

Who are we

Martin Floral Design (MFD) is a full service floral and event design company that specializes in creating high-style weddings and extraordinary events for the most discriminating of clients. Co-founder and owner Jet Martin ensures that each event is masterfully created and studded with gorgeous detail and unique charm. Whether beginning the process from the initial event concept or helping to develop an already chosen design aesthetic, MFD works closely with each client to ensure that their event is personalized and their expectations are surpassed.

Mission

To provide the finest and freshest quality in all products to our customers on a consistent and ongoing basis.

Vision

To be the most prominent San Diego floral supply in all aspects of the floral industry: quality, design, customer service, and achieving the goal of allowing customers to get flowers delivered today.
